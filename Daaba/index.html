Delicious Pie-Cake

Indulge in our signature pie-cake, a perfect blend of flaky crust and rich filling.

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  3. 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  4. 2 large eggs
  5. 1/2 cup milk
  6. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. 1 teaspoon baking powder
  8. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  9. 1 cup pie filling (apple, cherry, or your choice)
  10. Optional: powdered sugar

Delicious Kaju Kathli

Experience the rich taste of our Kaju Kathli, made with premium cashews and infused with aromatic spices.

  1. 1 cup cashew nuts (kaju)
  2. 1/2 cup sugar
  3. 1/4 cup water
  4. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder (optional)
  5. 1 teaspoon ghee (for greasing)
  6. Silver vark (optional for decoration)

Delicious Gulab Jamun

Savor the sweetness of our Gulab Jamun, soft and syrupy, made with the finest ingredients.

  1. 1 cup khoya (mawa)
  2. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida)
  3. 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  4. 2 tablespoons milk (as needed for dough)
  5. Oil or ghee (for deep frying)
  6. 1 cup sugar
  7. 3/4 cup water
  8. 4 cardamom pods (crushed)
  9. Few drops of rose water or saffron strands (optional)
